Salvador Dalí, the renowned surrealist artist, expanded his creativity beyond the canvas into the world of perfumery. His fragrances, such as Dalí, Dalissime, and Daliflor, embody the same surreal and imaginative spirit found in his artwork. These perfumes are a unique blend of notes that evoke the artist's fascination with dreams, sensuality, and the subconscious, offering a sensory experience that mirrors the surreal landscapes of his paintings.
